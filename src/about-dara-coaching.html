Values

Well-being Hour

Survey for organizations to gain valuable insights into team well-being. Anonymous and online for accurate data collection. Understand your team's needs to provide the necessary support.

Customised Workshops

An additional monthly service for remote/hybrid teams to prioritize their teams' well-being. A dedicated hour for each team member to focus on their well-being in a safe environment.

Well-being Survey

Customised workshops to support teams in reflecting on individual progress or finding common goals. Also suitable as meaningful activities to spend time together.
