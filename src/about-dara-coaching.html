About Life Coaching

1

How does it work?

Life coaching is a form of support aimed at helping individuals achieve their goals, overcome challenges and tap into their inner potential. The coach provides support, guidance and tools that help the client gain clarity on their values, objectives and dreams, while creating actionable steps to reach them.

The coaching process is grounded in deep listening, questioning and reflection. The coach assists the client in clarifying their thoughts, identifying obstacles and finding solutions. The client takes an active role in their own growth journey, working collaboratively with the coach to progress towards their set goals.

2

Why coaching?

Life coaching provides a framework for profound self-exploration and growth. It helps individuals gain clarity across different areas of life, enhance self-awareness and confidence and overcome feelings of uncertainty. Coaching also equips clients with practical tools and strategies that can be applied in their daily lives. It’s perfect for people that like to have concrete and actionable steps.

3

Benefits for individuals and businesses

For individuals, it offers personal growth, self-awareness and goal achievement. For businesses, coaching can lead to improved leadership, teamwork and job satisfaction. The shared objective for individuals and businesses is to achieve sustainable and balanced success.

FAQ

How do I know if I can benefit from a life coach?

Consider consulting a life coach if you could use practical, tailored guidance and support for your needs. Common scenarios include feeling stuck, uncertain about your life goals, navigating life transitions, managing stress and facing various challenges

What's the difference between life coaching and therapy?

Life coaching and therapy serve different purposes and use distinct approaches. Therapy typically focuses on addressing and healing emotional or psychological issues, often related to the past. It delves into understanding and managing mental health conditions and unresolved trauma.

Life coaching, on the other hand, concentrates on the present and future. It is goal-oriented and aimed at personal and professional development. Life coaches help clients set and achieve goals, enhance performance, and create a more fulfilling life, without addressing clinical issues or providing diagnoses and treatment.

What is the typical duration and frequency of coaching sessions?

The duration and frequency of coaching sessions can vary depending on your individual needs and goals. A general guideline is to have four 60-minute sessions, either bi-weekly or monthly. This helps ensure consistent progress and lasting change.

To determine the best session schedule for your specific situation, schedule a free introduction call where we can discuss your unique requirements in detail.

How do I choose the right life coach for me?

Choosing the right life coach is key to success. Define your goals, research coaches, interview them, assess experience and check references. Trust your instincts and ensure their approach aligns with your goals.
